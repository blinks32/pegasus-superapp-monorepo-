Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Developer Source Code Licenses

Due to the nature of digital goods (raw source code), we generally do not offer refunds once the code has been delivered. If you encounter issues during installation, our engineering support team is available to assist.

2. Enterprise Setup Services

A 50% refund is available if cancellation occurs before the design phase has begun. Once the engineering sprint starts, no refunds will be given for professional services.

3. How to Request a Refund

Please send an email to support@pegasussuperapp.com with your transaction details for any inquiries related to refunds.
